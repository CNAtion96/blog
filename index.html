Scott Pilgrim

Following the life of a young man from Toronto

Scott Pilgrim is Dating a High Shooler

Scott Pilgrim is now dating Knives Chau. Knives is a local Chinese Canadian that attends a Catholic School. Worry not dear reader, for they have only held hands. In fact neither of them have kissed a guy before.

Scott Sees the Girl of His Dreams

While spending time in the library with Knives Scott notices a young women by the front desk. They lock eyes and from that moment on he can't get her off his mind.

Scott Learns Her Name

At Julies party on friday Scott asked Kaumeau about a girl with pink hair. He told Scott her name was Ramona and he proceeded to ask half the party about her. That was up until Julie told him to drop it and that she doesn't want him scaring off the coolest girl at her party.

Scott Fails at Talking to Ramona

Shortly after his brush with Julie Scott spotted Ramona sitting at the edge of the party and proceeded to talk to her. He told her about Pac Man originally being called Puck Man. She gives him the cold shoulder so he leaves. He did however stalk her until she left the party.

Scott Asks Ramona on a Date

Scott found out from Julie that Ramona works at Amazon. Being the genius that he is Scott orders a package so she delivers it...because that's how that works. Turns out it is! She comes to the door and after a few minutes of akward conversation two things happen. First we learn that Ramona can travel through peoples minds. Second Scott is smooth as Joey Tribbiani. They have a date at 8.

Scott Sleeps With Ramona

On their date a big storm comes through. Ramona takes Scott home and offers him tea. He learns that there is more than one kind of tea. Soon they end up in the bedroom where things start to get steamy. Ramona however changes her mind about the intercourse, but she lets him stay the night. Before leaving the next morning Scott invites her to the battle of the bands that night.

First Evil Ex

At the battle of the bands one of Ramona's ex boyfriends attacks Scott during Sexbomb-omb's performance. Mathew Patel was Ramona's boyfriend in the seventh grade. He uses his mystical powers to fight Scott. Scott wins by punching him hard enough that he explodes into change.

Hiding From Knives

Having fallen in love with Ramona Scott is spending his time avoiding Knives until he gets the nerve to break up with her. He goes as far as jumping out of a window to prevent her from seeing him.

Breaking up with Knives

Having finally tracked him down in a phone booth Knives and scott go out to the local Goodwill. Knives confesses her love for Scott after asking him to come have dinner with her parents. Scott bruttally tells Knives that he doesn't want to be with her anymore. She is devistated.

New New Girlfriend

During band practice Scott informs the group that he is no long with Knives. Kim calles him the scum of the Earth for breaking up with her. He says not to worry because they will soon meet his new, new girlfriend. As if on summoned by his words Ramona knocks on the door. Scott is taken aback by her change in hair color, but none the less he abandons the band to run off with Ramona. Don't worry. Neil knows his part.
